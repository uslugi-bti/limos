Become a Chauffeur

Grow your income with Canadian Limos Inc.

Canadian Limos links certified chauffeurs with a nationwide clientele of business and leisure travellers. This opportunity allows you to supplement your existing schedule or even become your primary source of bookings. Our pricing is consistently aligned with local market standards, and our steadfast dedication to excellence ensures we maintain equitable rates for drivers while providing top-notch service to our customers.

On-Time Payments

We ensure prompt deposits of earnings from your completed rides are directly in your bank account.

Live Trip Tracking

With our user-friendly operating procedures, managing your rides becomes a breeze.

Flexible Scheduling

We provide various options, including city transfers, hourly reservations, intercity trips, and more.

Comprehensive Support

Our Partner Help Center offers 24/7 Support addressing ride-related inquiries.

Nationwide Network

Become part of Canada’s widest network of chauffeurs and limousines.

Embarking on a career as a chauffeur for Canadian Limos offers a unique blend of professionalism, elegance, and adventure. As a chauffeur, you become more than just a driver; you are a part of a luxurious experience, delivering impeccable service and comfort to clients. Behind the wheel of state-of-the-art vehicles, you navigate the roads with precision and grace, ensuring safe and stylish travel for every passenger.
Working with Canadian Limos, you represent a brand known for its commitment to excellence, where every journey is tailored to exceed expectations. This role is not just about driving; it’s about creating a memorable experience for the client, showcasing your dedication to professionalism, and enjoying the journey in the world of luxury transportation.

Requirements

  • 3 Years of Driving Experience in Canada
  • Clean Driving Record with Full Class License
  • Commercial Vehicle Insurance for Limousines
  • 2021 or Newer Luxury Vehicle
