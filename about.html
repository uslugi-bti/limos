About Us

Luxury Limousines & Chauffeur Services

Discover our story and commitment to excellence.

Canadian Limos is a Canada-based online booking platform providing premium transportation and professional chauffeur services for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and reliability.

We offer Canada’s elite chauffeur service with the latest fleet of luxurious vehicles to take our clients beyond regular travel to a premium business-class experience.

Why Choose Us

Discover the reasons that set us apart.

Culture

We understand your concerns and challenges better, being a part of the same society. Our team is here to accommodate your travel requests at all times, in your most exciting and challenging moments.

Local Business

We are here to assist in your local Canadian time zone with the right level of support at any time of the day. Our dedicated emergency remote support specialists are available during off-business hours.

Privacy

We respect your privacy and comply with the local Canadian privacy regulations. We never share your personal information with third-party marketers.

Support

Experience the difference in customer service, from the initial quote to the completion of the final service. We operate as your local business, available and ready to assist promptly and swiftly.

Technology-driven

With the aid of the most advanced technological systems and experienced team members, the margins of errors are minimized to 0.1% for accurate trip quotes, automated invoicing, secured online payment systems, trip scheduling and on-time pick-ups.

Trusted Elite Chauffeurs

Our chauffeur partners are commercially insured and licensed by the City (Local Municipality Transport Division) with a minimum driving experience of 10 years and over 1000 completed rides annually. We do not outsource trips to freelancing private drivers.

Local Canadian Business

We deeply understand your cultural concerns and challenges, as we are part of the same society and are committed to accommodating your travel needs during both exhilarating and challenging times. Being a local Canadian business, we offer assistance in your timezone, providing the right level of support whenever you need it, with dedicated emergency remote specialists available even during off-business hours. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we strictly adhere to Canadian privacy regulations, ensuring that your personal information is never shared with third-party marketers. Our customer support stands out, guiding you from the initial quote to the completion of our services, operating as your local business, always available to assist you promptly and efficiently.

Licensed Official Chauffeurs

Our service distinguishes itself through our team of licensed chauffeurs, officially recognized and accredited by the city. This licensing ensures that each chauffeur meets the high standards of safety, professionalism, and local knowledge required by city regulations. Unlike other businesses, our chauffeurs undergo rigorous background checks and continuous training to maintain their licenses, ensuring a reliable and secure customer experience. This commitment to excellence means you can trust our drivers to navigate the city with expertise and provide unmatched service and courtesy. With our licensed chauffeurs, you’re not just hiring a driver; you’re ensuring peace of mind, safety, and a premium travel experience tailored to your needs.

New Fleet of Luxury Vehicles

Our service stands out with its new fleet of luxury vehicles, setting us apart from competitors. Each vehicle in our fleet is a testament to modern luxury and comfort, ensuring an exceptional travel experience. Our cars are the latest models, equipped with cutting-edge technology, plush interiors, and advanced safety features. They are meticulously maintained and regularly updated to ensure they meet the highest standards of performance and luxury. This commitment to providing a new fleet means that our customers enjoy a level of sophistication and comfort that is rare in the industry. Whether it’s for business travel, a special occasion, or simply a desire for a premium travel experience, our luxury vehicles will redefine what you consider exceptional service in transportation.
